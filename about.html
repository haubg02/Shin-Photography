About Me

Sinh Tong Van

PHOTOGRAPHER / ENGINEER

No one rejects or avoids the beauty of nature just because it brings joy. Let us explore the night sky and admire the twinkling stars, as if sipping from the essence of celestial light. For no one can fully grasp all the secrets of the universe, but we can approach the truths discovered by astronomers, guiding us toward a life in harmony with nature.

It is important to experience the wonders of the cosmos, even when faced with challenges and difficulties. Every time we gaze upon a distant planet or a radiant nebula, we realize that the journey of exploration not only brings joy but also valuable lessons, sometimes requiring patience and effort. But no one should ever give up on discovery, for even in those challenges, we are rewarded by the vast and mysterious universe.

Experience

LAB 22 - Laboratory for the Creation of Intelligent Systems (Tokyo).

Focusing on research and development of the Santo robot with professor Gabriele Trovato and his colleagues. The project participates in the 2024 IEEE International Conference in Pasadena, California.

R&D CIMLAB (Hanoi)

I have experience in researching automatic machines, focusing on their development and implementation. Additionally, I provided support to team members in various technology competitions, contributing to innovative projects and technical problem-solving.

Astronomy club (Hanoi)

I have experience in sharing knowledge about astronomy, organizing competitions that focus on astronomical concepts and observations, and guiding individuals in building their own telescopes. These activities aim to inspire curiosity about the cosmos and encourage practical learning in the field of astronomy.

Skills

I have skills in designing robots, utilizing both technical and creative approaches to develop innovative robotic systems. Additionally, I am an amateur photographer, capturing moments and scenes that reflect my passion for visual storytelling.
